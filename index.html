[LAWSSA — 01]
OUR
RESPONSIBILITY

The Law Students' Association exists to serve, protect, and empower every law student at the University of Benin. Our functions span academic support, welfare advocacy, social organization, and professional development.

01
Academic Support & Resources

We curate and distribute past examination questions, textbooks, lecture notes, and legal reference materials across all levels — 100L through 500L — ensuring every student has access to the academic tools they need to excel. Our resource library is updated each semester by the Executive team.

02
Student Welfare & Advocacy

The welfare of law students is our highest priority. From academic grievances and examination irregularities to campus safety and faculty-student relations, LAWSSA stands as the first line of defence for every member. We engage the Faculty of Law and University management through structured dialogue and representation.

03
Social Events & Programmes

Beyond the lecture hall, LAWSSA organizes Law Week, annual dinners, inter-faculty debates, legal aid clinics, and social mixers that build lasting bonds among law students. We recognize that the social environment of a law student is as critical as their academic performance.

04
Professional Development

We bridge the gap between legal education and professional practice through seminars, law clinic partnerships, moot court coaching, and mentorship programmes connecting students with practising lawyers, judges, and legal professionals across Nigeria.

On behalf of the Executive Council of the Law Students' Association, University of Benin, I welcome you to our official digital platform — a space built to serve, inform, and connect every law student at UNIBEN.

Our mission this academic session is simple: to leave the association better than we found it. We are committed to enhancing academic access, improving welfare structures, and ensuring that every student from 100L to 500L feels represented, heard, and empowered. This platform is one of the many steps we have taken toward that commitment.

Whether you are here to pay your association dues, access academic resources, or stay informed about the latest news from your association — we are glad you are here. The law is not just what we study; it is how we lead.
