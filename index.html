OUR
RESPONSIBILITY

The Law Students' Association exists to serve, protect, and empower every law student at the University of Benin. Our functions span academic support, welfare advocacy, social organization, and professional development.

01
Academic Support & Resources

We curate and distribute past examination questions, textbooks, lecture notes, and legal reference materials across all levels — 100L through 500L — ensuring every student has access to the academic tools they need to excel. Our resource library is updated each semester by the Executive team.

02
Student Welfare & Advocacy

The welfare of law students is our highest priority. From academic grievances and examination irregularities to campus safety and faculty-student relations, LAWSA stands as the first line of defence for every member. We engage the Faculty of Law and University management through structured dialogue and representation.

03
Social Events & Programmes

Beyond the lecture hall, LAWSA organizes Law Week, annual dinners, inter-faculty debates, legal aid clinics, and social mixers that build lasting bonds among law students. We recognize that the social environment of a law student is as critical as their academic performance.

04
Professional Development

We bridge the gap between legal education and professional practice through seminars, law clinic partnerships, moot court coaching, and mentorship programmes connecting students with practising lawyers, judges, and legal professionals across Nigeria.

"Welcome Message from the President

It is with great pleasure and a profound sense of pride that I welcome you to the official website of the Law Students Association (LAWSA), University of Benin. The creation of this website marks a significant milestone in the history of our Association. It represents our commitment to embracing innovation, improving accessibility, and creating a more connected and informed LAWSA community.
This platform has been designed to serve as a central space where Lawsans, alumni, partners, and visitors can access important information about our Association, stay updated on our activities, explore opportunities, and connect with the various initiatives that continue to shape our community.
As an Association built on excellence, leadership, and service, we recognize the importance of adapting to the changing demands of our time. This website is not only a digital platform but also a reflection of our vision for a modern, transparent, and progressive LAWSA.
I extend my sincere appreciation to everyone whose dedication and efforts contributed to bringing this vision to life. Your commitment continues to strengthen the foundation upon which future generations of Lawsans will build.
As you navigate this platform, I invite you to engage, participate, and remain connected with the remarkable journey of the Law Students Association, University of Benin.
Welcome to a new era of LAWSA.
